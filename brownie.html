How to make brownies

Brownies are delectable treats and can be made in under an hour with this recipe~

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Set your oven to 180°C (350°F). Line a baking pan with baking paper or lightly grease it with butter.
  2. Melt the butter in a bowl (microwave or stovetop). Let it cool slightly.
  3. Add the sugar to the melted butter and stir until well combined.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time. Add the vanilla extract and mix until smooth.
  5. Sift in the flour and cocoa powder. Gently mix until just combined. Do not overmix, as this keeps the brownies fudgy.
  6. Pour the batter into your prepared pan and spread it evenly.
  7. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until the top looks set but the center is still slightly soft.
  8. Let the brownies cool before cutting for cleaner slices.
