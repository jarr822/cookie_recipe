How to make cookies

Cookies are sweet treats and can be made very easily with this recipe~

Ingredients:

  • flour
  • sugar
  • butter
  • eggs
  • vanilla
  • salt
  • baking soda
  • chocolate chips

Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
    2. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking soda, and salt.
    3. In another bowl, mix the butter and sugar together until it is smooth and creamy.
    4. Crack the eggs into the butter mixture and add the vanilla. Mix well.
    5. Gradually add the dry ingredients into the wet mixture and stir until a dough forms.
    6. Fold the chocolate chips evenly into the dough.
    7. Scoop small portions of dough and place them on a baking tray lined with baking paper.
    8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are lightly golden.
    9. Let the cookies cool for a few minutes before eating.
